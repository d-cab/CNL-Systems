Dilligence Tool

Request a demo

Dilligence Ai is an intelligent document analysis assistant designed to support finance and legal teams in the early stages of due diligence. Rather than replacing human expertise, it helps professionals move faster by providing a first-pass understanding of complex documents. Users can upload text-based PDFs — such as contracts, investment summaries, or compliance reports — and the tool will extract the text, analyze it using advanced language models, and return a concise summary of the contents. This helps teams identify areas that may require closer review, making the overall process more efficient and focused.

The tool is built to assist with repetitive and time-consuming tasks, like skimming long agreements for red flags or summarizing dense technical sections. It offers a faster way to gain insight into unfamiliar documents, highlight sections that deviate from norms, or prioritize what to review in detail. While Dilligence Ai is not a substitute for legal or financial judgment, it serves as a powerful supplement — giving analysts and advisors a clearer starting point. Designed with scalability in mind, the system can be integrated into internal workflows or used as a standalone assistant to help teams keep pace with growing documentation demands.
